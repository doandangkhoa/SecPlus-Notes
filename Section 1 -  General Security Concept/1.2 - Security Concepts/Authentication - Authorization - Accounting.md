# AAA framework
#### Identification
-  This is who you claim to be
-  Usually your username
### Authentication
-  Prove you are who you say you are
-  Password and other authentication factors
### Authorization
-  Based on your identification and authentication.
-  What access do you have?
### Accounting
-  Resources used: Login/Logout time, data sent/received.
![](../../Images/Pasted%20image%2020260501204154.png)

### Certificate Authentication
-  Most organizations maintain a trusted Certificate Authority (CA) (signed by a root CA).
-  The organization creates a certificate for a device (digital signature)
- The certificate can now be included on a device as an authentication factor

### Authorization Models
#### The user or device has now authenticated
-  To what do they now have access?
-  Time to apply an authorization model
