### Combination of principles
-  The fundamentals of security

| Confidentiality                                                          | Integrity                                | Availability          |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Prevent disclosure of information to unauthorized individuals or systems | Data can't be modified without detection | Systems and networks  |
#### Confidentiality
##### Certain information should only be known to certain people
-  Prevent unauthorized information disclosure
##### Encryption
-  Encode messages so only certain people can read
##### Access controls
-  Selectively  restrict access to a resource
##### Two-factor authentication
-  Additional conformation before information is disclosed
